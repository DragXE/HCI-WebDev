HISTORY

The concern of the founders of VOrskins is the proliferation of skin care and body care that contains ingredients that are harmful to the body and skin. Therefore, we created e-commerce that sells a variety of local beauty products, especially for the body and skin, which are vegan, cruelty-free, and use non-toxic ingredients

VISION

Selling locally made skin care and body care that is vegan, cruelty-free, and uses non-toxic ingredients for the health of the body and skin for anyone who buys and uses our products

MISSION

1. Cooperate with local brands with the best quality, which of course have vegan, cruelty-free, and use non-toxic ingredients
2. Provide the best service to consumers
3. Maintain product quality for the health and convenience of consumers
